# PriceTag Extension

![PriceTag Marquee](assets/small_promo_tile.png)

**PriceTag** is your automated, free price tracking assistant. Track any product on any website with a single click and get email alerts the second the price drops.

## Features
- **1-Click Tracking**: Just click the extension icon on any product page, highlight the price, and you're done. No accounts, no signups.
- **AI Powered**: Integrates directly with Google Gemini AI to bypass bot-blockers and intelligently scrape prices from the most stubborn modern websites.
- **Beautiful Email Alerts**: Get notified instantly via stunning HTML email alerts or native Chrome popups the exact moment your item goes on sale.
