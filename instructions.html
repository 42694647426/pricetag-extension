Welcome to PriceTag

Your step-by-step guide to mastering automated price tracking.

📌 How to Track a Product

  1. Open any product web page (Amazon, Walmart, BestBuy, etc.).
  2. Click the 🏷️ PriceTag extension icon in your browser toolbar.
  3. Click the Track Price on this Page button.
  4. Move your mouse over the specific price text on the screen. It will highlight in an intelligent blue box.
  5. Click the price! The extension will confirm the text it found and begin monitoring your item hourly.

📊 How to View Tracked Products

  1. Click the extension icon at the top of your browser.
  2. Select the Open Dashboard button.
  3. Your dashboard shows all your currently watched items, their original tracked prices, product thumbnails, and links back to the stores.

⚙️ Advanced Settings (Highly Recommended)

Inside the Dashboard, you have access to two powerful configuration panels:

  • Email Notifications: By default, the extension uses native computer popups when a price drops. Add your email address in the configuration panel to receive beautiful HTML email alerts seamlessly!
  • Gemini AI Integration: Modern retail websites constantly try to scramble their code to block simple price scrapers. Providing a free Gemini API key guarantees the extension can intelligently read even the most stubborn websites by using Google's AI to scan the page exactly like a human would!
